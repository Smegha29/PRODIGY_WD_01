Festivals by Season

Discover the vibrant celebrations across the globe, categorized by seasons and months of the year.

Chinese New Year

A colorful start to the year celebrated in China and beyond.

Harbin Ice Festival

China's grand winter spectacle featuring ice sculptures and art.

Rio Carnival

The world-famous carnival in Brazil, full of music and dance.

Venice Carnival

A glamorous festival in Italy with masks and grand costumes.

Holi

The festival of colors, celebrated across India to mark the arrival of spring.

St. Patrick's Day

An Irish celebration of heritage, marked by parades and green attire.

La Tomatina

A unique festival in Spain where participants throw tomatoes at each other.

Chuseok

South Korea's harvest festival, celebrating family and ancestral traditions.

Durga Puja

A grand celebration in India honoring Goddess Durga, marked by artistry and devotion.

Diwali

The festival of lights, celebrated in India during autumn.

Chhath Puja

A festival dedicated to the Sun God, celebrated in Bihar and other parts of India.

Day of the Dead

A Mexican tradition honoring loved ones who have passed away.

Christmas

A worldwide celebration of joy, marked by lights, gifts, and family gatherings.

Hanukkah

The Jewish festival of lights, celebrating perseverance and faith.
